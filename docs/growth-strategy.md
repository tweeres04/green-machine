# TeamStats Growth Strategy

**Created:** February 4, 2026

---

## Current State

### What's Working

TeamStats is growing organically through product-led growth:

1. **In-game visibility** — Tyler uses the app at games, actively tracking stats, asking teammates about assists. Everyone sees it in action.

2. **Social sharing** — Stats shared in team group chats via shareable cards. Creates awareness without being pushy.

3. **Word of mouth** — One teammate saw it, valued it, and bought it for her OTHER team. Pure product-driven conversion.

### First Organic Conversion (Feb 2026)

- Tyler uses TeamStats on two rec league teams
- A friend/teammate saw it in use
- She bought it for her separate team
- Zero marketing spend

**This is the PLG (product-led growth) pattern:**
1. One person uses it
2. Others see it / hear about it
3. They want it for their team
4. Repeat

## League Administrator Outreach (Big Opportunity)

**The idea:** Instead of acquiring one team at a time, partner with league administrators to get 10-20 teams at once.

### Questions to Explore

1. **Do rec leagues have administrators who make tool decisions?**
   - Who runs the leagues you play in?
   - Is there a central coordinator?

2. **What do they currently use for standings/stats?**
   - Spreadsheets? Nothing? A clunky legacy tool?
   - Is there pain here you could solve?

3. **Would leagues pay, or would teams pay individually?**
   - League-wide license vs. team-by-team
   - Different pricing models to consider

4. **Is there a "league season" timing?**
   - When do leagues start new seasons?
   - Best time to pitch new tools

5. **What would a league-level feature set look like?**
   - Cross-team standings
   - League-wide stats/leaderboards
   - Admin dashboard

### Potential Outreach Approach

1. Identify 2-3 local leagues to pilot
2. Talk to the league admin (cold outreach or warm intro)
3. Offer free trial or discount for feedback
4. Learn what they need
5. Build league features if validated
